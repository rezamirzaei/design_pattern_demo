üí° Pattern Logic
  • Target Interface: `SmartDevice` (methods: `turnOn()`, `getStatus()`).
  • Adaptee: `LegacyThermostat` (methods: `setTempSerial()`, `readVal()`).
  • Adapter: `ThermostatAdapter` implements `SmartDevice`, internally calls `LegacyThermostat` methods.
